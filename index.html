Bar Rista Cafe is a fresh take on contemporary American classics with a twist. We offer a laid-back neighborhood vibe paired with fresh, homemade food. We’ve decided to go back to the basics to give our neighborhood simple, good and consistent food and service. We are your local go-to for grabbing a sandwich, salad, beer or glass of wine. We know you’ll come back because of our quality, everyday menu items, and friendly, welcoming service.
